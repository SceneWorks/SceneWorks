Your workspace

Your projects, the images and videos you create, and any models you import or download from sites like Civitai are stored here. Pick a drive with plenty of free space.

Downloaded AI models

Large model files downloaded from Hugging Face are cached here. They're kept in a shared folder by default so other AI apps on your computer can reuse them — but you can store them anywhere, even inside your workspace folder.

You can change the model cache anytime; moving your workspace later needs a restart.
